"MARSHMALLOW CHALLENGE😎"

The Marshmallow Challenge is a team-building activity where my group and I are given spaghetti sticks, tape, string, and a marshmallow. We must build the tallest structure with the marshmallow on top. This teaches teamwork, creativity, and fast problem-solving.
